How to Use

To apply premium text selection to any element, simply add one of these classes:

  • selection-luxury-black - The most premium option
  • selection-premium-black - Classic professional
  • selection-premium-dark - Modern dark theme
  • selection-elite-black - Exclusive elite styling
  • selection-midnight-black - Ultimate darkness
